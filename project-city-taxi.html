City Taxi Management System

The City Taxi Management System offers a user-friendly interface for both drivers and passengers, ensuring seamless ride bookings, efficient management of driver details, and real-time tracking of rides. Key features include a responsive design, lightbox for additional information, statistics for critical metrics, testimonial sliders, logo carousels, and fully functional forms to facilitate user interactions and feedback.
