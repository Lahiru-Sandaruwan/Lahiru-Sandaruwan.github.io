Features include an eye-catching morph text in the header, details lightbox for more information, statistics numbers for important values, card slider for testimonials, image slider for customer logos, and working forms that will enable your landing page to provide leads in order to achieve your marketing goals.

New elements added to the package

Some useful extra pages are bundled with the template like article details, terms, conditions, and privacy policy which can be customized as per your requirements. The City Taxi Management System features a modern design combined with functional elements to enhance user experience.

The City Taxi Management System is an easy-to-customize HTML template built with Bootstrap for promoting taxi services to the online audience and ensuring seamless ride bookings. This template is built with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to ensure the highest flexibility for all users.

Visitors love a beautiful and efficient website

All designers, developers, and tech-savvy people will be able to customize this template with basic web coding skills. Among the features you will find details lightbox for more information, tabbed content for feature details, video lightbox, card slider for testimonials, statistics numbers, image slider for customer logos, dropdown navigation, and useful extra pages.

With a modern design and intuitive interface, the overall landing page design will showcase the taxi service beautifully and will help in convincing visitors to book rides effortlessly. The layout maximizes the impact of your content with engaging visuals and informative elements.

  • Technology Stack: Java, Spring Boot, MySQL
  • Project Overview: A complete city taxi management system designed to streamline ride bookings, manage payments, and handle customer and driver interactions effectively.
  • Key Features: Real-time booking, Driver management, Fare calculation
  • Challenges and Solutions: Handling high concurrency booking requests and ensuring a smooth user experience through efficient database management.
  • Outcome: Enabled a seamless booking experience for customers and improved operational efficiency for drivers.
